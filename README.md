# Augmented-Reality-
Augmented Reality With 3D Models 

This 3D Augmented Reality (AR) application showcases the beauty and cultural diversity of Indonesia through interactive visualizations of major islands such as Kalimantan, Sulawesi, Java, and Papua. Each island is presented as a detailed 3D model, allowing users to explore its geographical shape in a more realistic way.

On each island, there are representations of traditional houses unique to each region, such as the longhouse from Kalimantan, Tongkonan house from Sulawesi, Joglo house from Java, and Honai house from Papua. In addition, there are human figures dressed in traditional clothing, providing a more vivid and educational representation of local cultures.

Through this AR technology, users can directly interact with the 3D objects by zooming, rotating, and exploring every detail. This application aims to increase interest in learning about Indonesian culture in a modern, engaging, and easy-to-understand way, especially for younger generations.
